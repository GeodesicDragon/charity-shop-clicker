Version 1.11.39 (April 27th 2026)

* Added offline earnings.
	* When you save your game, the current date and time are also saved.
	* When you next load your save, the length of time you were away for is calculated, and you are given the appropriate amount of money. For example, if you were earning 100 money per second and were away from the game for two days, which is 172,800 seconds, you would be given 17,280,000 on load.
	* Offline progress is limited to a maximum of seven days.

Version 1.0.0 (March 3rd 2026)

* Added setting to change which currency is being used.
	* Right now you can choose between British Pounds, US Dollars, or Euros.
	* More currencies may be added in later updates.
* Added three new departments.
	* Toys and Games: Has a starting price of 100,000 and adds 75 per second.
	* New Goods: Has a starting price of 150,000 and adds 100 per second.
	* Electricals: Has a starting price of 200,000 and adds 150 per second.
* Added three new upgrades.
	* All Pieces Present: Has a starting price of 1500 and increases output of toys and games by 10% each time it is purchased.
	* Unique Gifts: Has a starting price of 2000 and increases output of new goods by 10% each time it is purchased.
	* Plug and Play: Has a starting price of 2500 and increases output of electricals by 10% each time it is purchased.
* Added autoclicker upgrade.
	* Starting price is 10,000 with a 10 second click delay.
	* Each subsequent purchase reduces this by one second.
	* Upon reaching one click per second, the button is disabled.
* Fixed money per second calculation error when purchasing new departments.
* Removed the automatic update checker, as it was a bit temperamental. Instead, you can now go to the latest GitHub release from the 'Game' menu.

With the remaining departments and upgrades added, I feel it's time to come out of the Alpha stage and officially mark this project as completed. That doesn't mean I'm going to stop working on it, though; I will continue to fix bugs and tweak things based on any feedback I might get.

Version 0.6.13-Alpha (January 25th 2025)

* Added events.
	* Events have a 10% chance of occurring every ten minutes, and are chosen at random.
	* The possible events are:
		* PR Success: +50% money per second for 5 seconds.
		* PR Disaster: -50% money per second for 5 seconds.
		* Ringing Registers: +75% money per second for 10 seconds.
		* Sticky Fingers: -75% money per second for 10 seconds.
	* Once the event ends, your money per second is restored to its pre-event value.
	* Any departments or upgrades bought during the event will be taken into account, so no progress will be lost.
* Fixed the update checker incorrectly informing you of new versions when there weren't any.
* Removed tooltips from department buttons; they now show how much money per second they bring in.
* Fixed the 'Good Plates' upgrade not working as intended (I forgot to actually code it).
* Fixed the Latest Releases upgrade not working as intended (again, I forgot to code it).
* Fixed the Latest Releases upgrade showing an incorrect tooltip.
* Increased the bonus given by the Good Plates upgrade to 10% (was 1%).
* Increased the bonus given by the Latest Releases upgrade to 10% (was 1%).
